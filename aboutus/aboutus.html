    • Team Project

    These days, people are increasingly looking to order food from the comfort of their own home. The pricing in food delivery apps typically include the menu item, a service fee, taxes, a delivery fee, and often, gratuity. There can be a significant difference between the prices when ordering through a food delivery app and ordering directly from the restaurant. We want to provide affordability to our customers while making sure restaurants are able to keep the profits they earn.

    As a group we are working on developing a food ordering website. We are looking to compete agaisnt the top companies in our sector, which include Uber, DoorDash, GrubHub, among others. We are looking to grow by directly impacting User experience and problems when it comes to food ordering websites.

    We will go into an indepth explanation of our project and what it entails
    We are developing a Website for our service called FastPlate, we are offering a modern minimalist website design to engage the user
    Flexibilty is something we are working on to give the user the most customizable experince they can get, Like our competitors we are offering a delivery service with our website, we want to give our users the best experince when ordering from us. We want to give multiple delivery options which include contacless delivery, specific time delivery, and an option for the user to input any additional information
    What differentiates use from our competitors is that we want to take out service fees, reduce the delivery fee, and tip, by giving our drivers a based salary.
    Our main priority for this project is Consumer Satisfaction, we are prioritizing our food delivery time and prices for the optimal consumer satisfaction.
